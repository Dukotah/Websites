How a project goes

Straightforward, start to finish.

  1. 01

    Walk the site

    We come out, look at the slope, the drainage and how you want to use the space, and talk through what stone and materials fit.

  2. 02

    Design & quote

    You get a clear plan and a written estimate — scope, materials and timeline, with no pressure to decide on the spot.

  3. 03

    Grade & build

    One crew handles demo, grading, drainage and the build — walls, patios and stonework set to last, kept clean as we go.

  4. 04

    Walk-through

    We finish the planting and finishes, then walk the project with you and make sure it's right before we call it done.
