Our care

Everything one family needs — under one roof.

From a child's first checkup to a confident new smile, our three dentists cover the full range of general, cosmetic and family dentistry — so you can keep the same trusted team for life.

Cleanings & prevention

Gentle exams, hygiene cleanings and preventive care that keep small problems small — the foundation of a healthy smile for life.

Cosmetic & whitening

Professional teeth whitening and cosmetic dentistry to brighten and refine your smile, tailored to what looks natural for you.

Invisalign clear aligners

Straighten your teeth discreetly with clear, removable aligners — a comfortable alternative to traditional metal braces.

Veneers & crowns

Porcelain veneers and crowns that restore strength and appearance, color-matched to blend seamlessly with your own teeth.

Family & pediatric

From a child's first visit to multi-generation families, Dr. Ryan and the team make every age feel at ease in the chair.

Emergency dental care

Tooth pain or a broken tooth? Call the office — we make room to see established and new patients when something urgent comes up.

Meet the dentists

Three dentists who genuinely like people.

Between them, decades of training and a shared belief that dentistry should feel calm, kind and unhurried — for grandparents and grade-schoolers alike.

DY

Dr. Donna Yock, DMD

Founder · General & cosmetic dentistry

Earned her DMD from the University of Medicine & Dentistry of New Jersey in 1984 and has cared for Rohnert Park families since 1988. A member of the ADA, CDA and Redwood Empire Dental Society, she has served on the Education Foundation of Rohnert Park & Cotati and as an ambassador for the local Chamber of Commerce.

EK

Dr. Eddie Kuo, DDS

General dentistry · partner

Studied biological sciences at UC Davis before earning his DDS from the University of the Pacific, Arthur A. Dugoni School of Dentistry, followed by a general-practice residency at Erie County Medical Center (SUNY Buffalo). He volunteers widely — Give Kids a Smile, CDA Cares, Project Homeless Connect and more.

SR

Dr. Susie Ryan, DDS

Pediatric & family dentistry

A graduate of the University of the Pacific School of Dentistry who served as chief resident in both a general-dentistry residency in Fresno and a pediatric residency in Buffalo, NY. She is affiliated with the AAPD and brings gentle, kid-friendly care to the practice's youngest patients.
